World Development Indicators

We are interested in exploring the Sustainable Development Goals set forth by The World Bank that they would like to achieve by 2030. These goals are measured by several World Development Indicators. We will select several indicators to help predict the development of a country as measured by the Human Development Index.

HDI

The Human Development Index is a comprehensive measure of the development of a country that takes into account factors other than just GDP such as health, education, infrastructure, etc.
