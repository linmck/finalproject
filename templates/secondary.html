Education Indicators

How Education plays a role in the Human Development Index.

To the left you will find a chart demonstrating the relationship between Education Index and Human Development Index. You can see that there is a clear linear relationship between a country's Education Index and its Human Development Index.

By striving to increase the availability of basic education and encouraging advanced education for all citizens, countries can potentially increase their overall HDI. To the right you may scroll through select indicators that countries could focus goals around to increase their overall Human Development Index.
