Primary Indicators

Life-expectancy, National Income, and Agriculture defines HDI.

Life-expectancy, National Income (GNI), and Agriculture has a strong correlation with Nation's Human Development Index (HDI). Higher life-expectancy and national-income indicated higher HDI. On contrary, higher Agricultural contribution to GDP suggested lower HDI.

Nation's employment (%) do not define HDI. Therefore, life-expectancy, GNI and Agricultural contribution to GDP could be used to build a model to predict HDI.
